Each panel = dismissible card hovering over the canvas. Toggle dots top-right show/hide each. Cards drag freely on desktop; on mobile they go fullscreen-modal when tapped. Keep the current intent of PR #19 / f9af4ea; rip out the dead drawer CSS.
