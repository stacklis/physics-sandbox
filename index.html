Questions

What devices does it run on?

Any modern desktop or mobile browser. Tested on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. There's also an Android app via Google Play (coming v1.1) that wraps the web build.

Does it work offline?

Yes — install it as a PWA ("Add to Home Screen" on mobile, or the install icon in your desktop browser's address bar) and it runs locally with no network needed.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund within 14 days, no questions asked. Email support@stacklis.com with your Stripe receipt and the refund goes through within a business day.

Is there a classroom or site license?

The $9 one-time covers personal use across your devices. For classroom or school-wide deployments, email support@stacklis.com — we're happy to work something out, including teacher comp licenses.

What data do you collect?

None. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. Pro state lives in your browser's localStorage. Stripe handles payment and never shares card data with us. Full details in the privacy policy.

Where's the Android app?

Coming as v1.1. The web app installs as a Progressive Web App on Android in the meantime — Chrome will offer the install prompt, or use the menu's "Install app" option.

Is it open source?

The client-side simulator is open and inspectable on GitHub (link in the footer). The Pro unlock is honor-system: we trust you. If the simulator is useful to you, the $9 pays for ongoing maintenance.

Will the price go up?

Maybe, eventually, for new buyers. If you've already bought Pro, you keep it at no extra cost — including all v1 updates.
