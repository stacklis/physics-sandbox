Questions

What devices does it run on?

Any modern desktop or mobile browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. There's also an Android app via Google Play (coming v1.1) that wraps the same web build.

Does it work offline?

Yes — install it as a PWA ("Add to Home Screen" on mobile, or the install icon in your desktop browser's address bar) and it runs completely locally with no network needed.

When is the Android app available?

Coming as v1.1 on Google Play. Until then, the PWA installs as a home-screen app on Android via Chrome — tap the three-dot menu and choose "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen."

What's the refund policy?

Full refund within 14 days, no questions asked. Email support@stacklis.com with your Stripe receipt and the refund goes through within a business day.

The $9 one-time covers personal use across your own devices. For classroom or school-wide deployments, email support@stacklis.com — we're happy to work something out, including teacher comp licenses.

What data do you collect?

None. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. Pro state lives in your browser's localStorage. Stripe handles payment and never shares card data with us. See the privacy policy for full details.
