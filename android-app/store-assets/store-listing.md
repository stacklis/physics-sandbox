## Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
Physics Sandbox is a 2D rigid-body physics simulator that explains itself while you play.

Drag in empty space to spawn objects — balls, boxes, polygons, walls, ropes, triangles. Watch them collide, bounce, swing, and fall under real physics: SAT collision detection, impulse-momentum solving, Coulomb friction, distance and spring constraints.

The moment you touch the canvas, live readouts appear: speed, kinetic energy, potential energy, momentum, angular velocity — for the selected body and for the whole system. You can watch energy conservation play out in real time.

FOUR KNOWLEDGE LEVELS

Switch between four explanation tiers without pausing the simulation:

• Curious — plain-language descriptions. "Things with more mass need more push to get moving."
• Student — introductory physics language. Covers Newton's laws, work-energy theorem, momentum.
• University — undergraduate mechanics vocabulary. Impulse-momentum theorem, normal force, torque tensors.
• Expert — graduate-level formulation. Lagrangian mechanics, impulse-momentum in matrix form, coefficient of restitution derivations.

Same simulation, different vocabulary. Great for students reviewing concepts, teachers preparing class demos, and curious adults who want to see the math behind the motion.

PRESETS

Launch any of eight built-in scenes with one tap:
Stack · Pendulum · Newton's Cradle · Ramp · Orbital system · Dominoes · Tower · Wrecking ball

CONTROLS

Six spawn tools: Box, Ball, Polygon, Wall, Triangle, Rope.
Six interact tools: Grab, Spring, Push, Delete, Pin, Slice.
Eight materials: Default, Rubber, Ice, Metal, Wood, Bouncy, Heavy, Light.

Adjust gravity (−20 to +40 m/s²), time scale (0× to 2×), and toggle destruction mode for satisfying demolition runs.

PRO FEATURES — $9 one-time

• Save your scenes to a JSON file and reload them on any device.
• Export your encountered physics concepts as a printable HTML document — university and expert-level explanations with full formula derivations, ready to print or share.

The Pro unlock is a single one-time payment of $9 USD. No subscription, no ads, no data collection. Works fully offline.

PRIVACY

No accounts. No analytics. No telemetry. The free edition and Pro both run entirely in your browser/device. Pro state lives in local storage. Payment is handled by Stripe on their secure servers — we never see your card.

Full privacy policy: https://physics.stacklis.com/privacy.html

ALSO AVAILABLE AS A WEB PWA

Install Physics Sandbox from https://physics.stacklis.com in any modern browser. The Android app and the web app are the same codebase — scenes saved on one work on the other.
```
